Demolition for Guthrie's Older Homes

Guthrie sits in Logan County, a manageable drive south of our Stillwater base. The town is known for its historic district and its blocks of century-old homes, and that shapes a lot of the work we do here.

Renovating an old home almost always starts with demo — dated kitchens and bathrooms pulled out, old flooring and plaster removed, sagging porches and decks taken down. We work carefully around the parts you're keeping, contain the mess, and haul off every load so your contractor walks into a clean space. We're not historic restorers, but we're the crew that clears the way for them.

We also do the everyday work — shed tear-downs, fence removal, property cleanouts, and junk hauling — anywhere in the Guthrie area.
